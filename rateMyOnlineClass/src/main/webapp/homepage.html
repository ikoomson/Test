What is RateMyOnlineClass?

This website was created to inquire into the online learning experiences of different student demographics at many institutions of learning. To help this project, you can leave a review for an online course that you are currently, or were previously enrolled in. You can also access course reviews by other students using the 'Search for a Class Review' feature and view student demographic information using the 'View Survey Taker Demographic' feature.
